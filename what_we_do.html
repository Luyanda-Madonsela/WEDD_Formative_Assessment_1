Meet Lucy, one of our many success stories...

We received a call about a dog that had been abandoned in a park. When we arrived, we found a small, scared dog huddled under a bench. The dog was covered in dirt and fleas, and she was very thin. We took her in and cared for her. We also gave her a name, Lucy. Lucy was very shy at first, but she slowly started to come out of her shell. She loved to play with the other dogs at the shelter, and she was always happy to see the staff. After a few weeks, Lucy was ready to be adopted. A wonderful family came to the shelter and fell in love with her. They took her home that day, and she has been living happily ever after ever since. Lucy's story is a reminder that there are many animals out there who need our help. There are so many wonderful animals out there who are waiting for their forever home.
